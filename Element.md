Elements:

| **Element** | **Nature** | **Creature** |
|:------------|:-----------|:-------------|
| [Air](Air.md)     |  Gases     |  Breath      |
| [Water](Water.md)   |  Fluids    |  Blood       |
| [Earth](Earth.md)   |  Solids    |  Flesh       |
| [Fire](Fire.md)    |  Energy    |  Life        |

Derived elements:
